Lasagna

Description

Here you will find all the things needed to create a delicious lasagna, that is both enjoyable to cook and eat!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese

Steps

  • Make the meat sauce.
  • Cook the noodles.
  • Make the ricotta mixture.
  • Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  • Cover with foil and bake.
  • Let the lasagna rest before serving.
