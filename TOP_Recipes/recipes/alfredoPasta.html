Alfredo Pasta

Description

Here you will find all the things needed to create a delicious alfredo pasta, that is both enjoyable to cook and eat!

Ingredients

  • Chicken patty
  • Pasta
  • Butter and Cream
  • Cheese

Steps

  • Toss the pasta in the cheese sauce
  • Melt the butter and cream together on the stove, season, and stir in the cheese.
  • Cook the pasta.
